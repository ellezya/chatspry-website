Common questions

What happens when I hit the free tier limit?
You will see a message letting you know you have used all 10 free saves for the month. Your saved chats stay safe. The counter resets at the start of each month.
Can I cancel my Pro subscription?
Yes — cancel any time from your Lemon Squeezy account page. No questions asked, no cancellation fees.
Does ChatSpry store my conversations?
No. ChatSpry reads your chat from the page and sends it directly to your Google Drive or your device. We never store your conversation content on our servers.
Which AI platforms are supported?
Claude (claude.ai), ChatGPT (chatgpt.com), and Gemini (gemini.google.com). All three are included on both Free and Pro.
Do I need a Google account?
Only if you want to save to Google Drive. You can use ChatSpry to download files to your device or copy to clipboard without connecting Google at all.
